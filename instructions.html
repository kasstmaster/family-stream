Follow the steps below to watch with Plex and request new titles through Jellyseerr.

Android TV / Google TV / Fire TV

  • Open the Play Store (or Amazon Appstore on Fire TV) and install Plex.
  • Open Plex and sign in with your Plex account.
  • Go to “Home” and you should see our library after you’re shared in.
    • Troubleshooting tips
    • Can't see the library? Sign out/in again, then open “Sources” and check that our server is selected.
    • Clock mismatch: Make sure your device’s date/time is correct.
    • Network limits: Some guest/hotel Wi-Fi blocks streaming.

    Roku (Plex)

    You have two ways to watch Plex on Roku:

    Option 1 – Sign in on Roku

  • From the Channel Store, install Plex.
  • Open Plex, choose Sign In, and follow the code pairing (https://app.plex.tv).
  • Once signed in, our shared library will appear automatically.

    • Option 2 – Cast from your phone

  • Install the Plex app on your Roku (no sign-in required on Roku).
  • On your phone, open Plex and connect to our server.
  • Tap Cast and select your Roku.

    • Phone and Roku must be on the same Wi-Fi.

    Troubleshooting tips
    • No library? Check the Plex account that was shared.
    • Cast button missing? Same Wi-Fi on both devices, update apps.
    • Update Roku: Settings → System → System update → Check now.

    Requesting New Titles

    Use Request Movies & Shows on your phone/tablet/computer. TV apps can’t request.

    Need help? Take a photo of your screen and send it to us.
