Follow the steps below to connect to Plex and request new titles through Jellyseerr.

Plex Access

On your TV, phone, or computer, open the Plex app and sign in with your Plex account. Remote access is built in — no manual server URL is required.

Plex Web App: https://app.plex.tv

Requesting New Titles

Use Jellyseerr on your phone, tablet, or computer. Visit the following URL, search for a title, and tap Request.

Jellyseerr URL: https://discover.ilymmd.com

Requests must be made through this site, not through TV apps.

Need help? Take a photo or screenshot and send it to us. We'll get you connected fast.
